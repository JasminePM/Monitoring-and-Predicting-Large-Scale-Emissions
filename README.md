### Monitoring and Predicting Large Scale Flue Gas Emissions
Monitoring large-scale emissions in a period of heightened concern to Climate Sensitivity is an imperative effort to ensuring environmental protections and industrial compliance relative to air quality. Additional motivations for studying these bioproducts referenced in full report -- (Operational Optimization, Energy Efficiency, Equipment Protection). The selected dataset contains records from a Turkish gas turbine measuring flue gas emissions (CO and NOx) over the course of one hour via sensors.
